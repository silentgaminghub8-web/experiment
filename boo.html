ANTI-HACK DEPARTMENT

1. System Overview

The face of our anti-hack system is Marjin Boo. Our team monitors every match. If a hacker is found, a rematch is conducted within 30 minutes. The hacker is suspended, and the player who provides valid proof receives a reward.

2. Match Monitoring & Roles

Two official Marjin members spectate every match. Sometimes, players are also designated as "Player-Marjin" to record and report any suspicious activity if they are eliminated by a hacker.

3. Penalty & Reward System

Hackers are suspended immediately. If a Marjin's recording leads to catching a hacker, the hacker's entry fee is directly transferred to the Marjin's wallet after admin verification.
